Some Important Notice about Fanta

Fanta is a popular fruit-flavored carbonated soft drink brand owned by The Coca-Cola Company. It was originally created in Germany during World War II in 1940, when trade restrictions made it difficult to import Coca-Cola syrup. As a result, the German Coca-Cola bottling company developed Fanta using available ingredients like whey and apple fiber.
